# Founder Notes

## Product Vision

ProfitLens helps Etsy sellers turn messy official CSV exports into a clean profit report they can understand, download, and share with a CPA.

The long-term vision is to become the simplest financial clarity tool for small online sellers. The MVP starts with only Etsy and only the profit report workflow.

## Why Now

Etsy sellers increasingly rely on ads, shipping labels, marketplace tax collection, refunds, and platform fees. These items are spread across exports and are hard to understand during tax season.

Many small sellers are not ready for full accounting software. They need a narrow tool that produces a practical report quickly.

## Positioning

ProfitLens is positioned as:

- CSV-first.
- Etsy-only at MVP.
- Seller-friendly.
- CPA-ready.
- Lower friction than accounting sync tools.
- Easier to understand than raw spreadsheets.

One-line positioning:

> Upload Etsy CSV, get a CPA-ready profit report.

## Founder Rules

- Talk to Etsy sellers before expanding features.
- Validate usage early before adding payment friction.
- Keep the first free report workflow narrow.
- Use customer confusion as the roadmap.
- Build only what supports the first dollar of revenue.
- Prefer manual support over complex automation in the first 90 days.

## What We Do Not Build Now

- No Shopify support.
- No Amazon support.
- No AI assistant.
- No API or webhook platform.
- No team workspace.
- No bookkeeping firm portal.
- No inventory management.
- No tax filing.
- No official tax advice.
- No real-time Etsy API integration in MVP.
- No forced payment gate before default MVP report download.
